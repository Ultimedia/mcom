About Pharmaceutical Press

MedicinesComplete is produced by Pharmaceutical Press, the publishing division of the Royal Pharmaceutical Society.

Pharmaceutical Press prides itself on its rigorous processes, editorial expertise and innovative approach to content delivery. The principal publishing focus is on the design, manufacture and therapeutic use of medicines, as well as the professional concerns of those working to ensure their safe administration and use.

To buy any Pharmaceutical Press products, please visit the Pharmaceutical Press store.
